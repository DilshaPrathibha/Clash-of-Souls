## üìñ About the Game

Clash of Souls is a magical fantasy battle game featuring intense 4v4 team gameplay. Control your character, collect mystical spirit flames scattered across enchanted maps, and bring them back to your base while your AI teammates help you battle against the opposing team!

### Key Features

- **Team-Based Gameplay**: 4v4 battles with Green vs Purple teams
- **Spirit Flame Collection**: Gather 80 mystical spirit flames scattered across magical maps
- **Power-Ups System**: Collect magical power-ups for speed boosts, magnet abilities, invincibility, and night vision
- **Multiple Enchanted Maps**: Switch between Classic Graveyard, Haunted Mansion, and Mystical Forest themes
- **Character Customization**: Choose your character type and customize with achievement hats
- **Progressive Achievement System**: Unlock special hats by completing challenges
- **Stats Tracking**: Track your total flames, games played, scores, and power-ups used
- **Dynamic AI Allies & Opponents**: Intelligent AI characters with strategic behaviors
- **Steal Mechanics**: Get close to enemy characters to steal their collected flames

## üéØ How to Play

- **Movement**: Use Arrow keys to control your character
- **Objective**: Collect mystical spirit flames and return them to your team's base
- **Strategy**: Avoid enemies when carrying flames, or steal from their tails by getting close
- **Power-Ups**: Collect magical items for temporary advantages (speed, magnet, invincibility, night vision)
- **Achievement Hats**: Unlock cosmetic hats by reaching milestones (e.g., collect 50 flames for Flame Collector hat)
- **Map Switching**: Change between different themed maps during gameplay
- **Win Condition**: The team with the most flames after 2 minutes wins!
